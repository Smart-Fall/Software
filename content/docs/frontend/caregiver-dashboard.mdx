# Caregiver Dashboard

Patient monitoring and care coordination dashboard for caregivers.

## Key Features

### Patient List
- All assigned patients
- Quick health status
- Filter/search capabilities

### Alert Management
- Active alerts for all patients
- High-priority notifications
- Acknowledge/respond workflows

### Fall Events
- Timeline of patient falls
- Confidence levels
- Response history

### Device Monitoring
- Device status per patient
- Battery levels
- Last data timestamp

## Dashboard Widgets

### Active Alerts Card
```
2 Active Alerts
- Jane Doe: High fall (2:30 PM)
- John Smith: Low battery (1:15 PM)
[View All] [Manage Alerts]
```

### Patient Health Summary
```
5 Patients
4 Low Risk
1 High Risk
[Manage Patients]
```

### Recent Activity
Timeline of all patient events and alerts
