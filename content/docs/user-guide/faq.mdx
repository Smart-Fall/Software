# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is SmartFall?

SmartFall is an advanced fall detection and health monitoring system designed to help you live independently while keeping your safety a priority. It combines wearable sensors with a mobile/web application to detect falls in real-time and alert caregivers.

### How does SmartFall detect falls?

SmartFall uses AI-powered accelerometer and gyroscope sensors in the wearable device to detect the characteristic patterns of a fall. The system analyzes acceleration, velocity, and impact to distinguish falls from normal activities like sitting down quickly.

### Is SmartFall a medical device?

SmartFall is a health monitoring tool designed to assist with fall detection and health tracking. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice or emergency services. Always call 911 for emergencies.

### Who should use SmartFall?

SmartFall is ideal for:

- Older adults at risk of falls
- Individuals with mobility challenges
- People recovering from surgery or illness
- Anyone wanting to maintain independence with safety oversight

## Account & Setup

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit SmartFall and click "Sign Up"
2. Choose your user type (Patient or Caregiver)
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Complete your profile information
5. Pair your device (if you're a patient)

### What's the difference between Patient and Caregiver accounts?

- **Patient:** Uses the device, sees personal health data, can communicate with caregivers
- **Caregiver:** Monitors multiple patients, receives alerts, can view health data with permission

### Can I switch between Patient and Caregiver roles?

Not on the same account. Create a separate account for each role you need.

### How do I add a caregiver?

1. Log in to your patient account
2. Go to Settings or Profile
3. Select "Add Caregiver"
4. Enter their email address
5. They'll receive an invitation to accept

### Can I have multiple caregivers?

Yes, you can add multiple caregivers from family, friends, or healthcare providers.

## Fall Detection & Alerts

### How quickly does SmartFall alert caregivers?

Fall detection typically occurs within 1-2 seconds of the event. Caregiver notifications are sent immediately.

### Can I manually alert my caregiver?

Yes, you can press and hold the emergency button on your device to send an immediate alert, even if no fall is detected.

### What happens if I fall but the device thinks I'm OK?

If you've fallen but the device hasn't detected it, you can manually trigger an alert. Conversely, you can dismiss false alarms.

### Can false alarms occur?

While our detection is highly accurate, false positives can occur during rapid movements. You can dismiss false alarms with a simple tap.

### How is my location determined during an alert?

SmartFall uses GPS, WiFi, and cell tower triangulation for location. Your exact location is sent to caregivers during emergencies.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my health data secure?

Yes, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with HIPAA, GDPR, and other healthcare privacy regulations.

### Who can see my health data?

Only you and caregivers you've explicitly authorized. Healthcare providers require separate consent.

### Can I delete my data?

Yes, you can request data deletion. Certain health records may be retained per legal requirements. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Is my location always tracked?

No, your location is only accessed during emergencies or when you enable location sharing. Premium features allow continuous tracking with your consent.

## Troubleshooting & Support

### My device won't connect to the app

1. Restart both the device and your phone
2. Ensure Bluetooth is enabled
3. Remove the device from existing pairings and re-pair
4. Restart the SmartFall app
5. If issues persist, contact support

### I'm not receiving alerts

1. Check that notifications are enabled in app settings
2. Verify caregiver email addresses are correct
3. Check spam folders for notifications
4. Ensure your phone has a strong internet connection
5. Re-enable notifications in your device settings

### The app crashes frequently

1. Update the app to the latest version
2. Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > SmartFall > Clear Cache)
3. Restart your phone
4. Reinstall the app if issues continue
